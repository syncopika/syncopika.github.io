I'm Nicholas. My Chinese name is "熊志文". Interestingly, my family name is the character used for "bear", as in the animal.

I really like music composition and programming.


I often use 'syncopika' as my 'artist' name. It's a combination of syncopation (I really like syncopated rhythms) and 'pika' (Japanese onomatopoeia for something sparkly).
