about this website:

hi, thanks for visiting!


this place is just mainly to practice using angular.js (and to practice writing I guess)

I enjoy watching Japanese animation and listening to the music they have.

this website sort of acts as a log for the soundtracks I have heard from the anime I have watched.


ideally, I'd like to have a physical collection too one day! ^_^


by the way, i'm using the 'ms gothic' font family because it's kinda neat and I think this font is often associated with (or at least very similar to) the english text produced with japanese keyboards.

also, the images for the soundtracks come from vgmdb, a really great resource for more info such as staff/personnel who worked on the soundtracks and track names.
